#### _**Prior Planning***_

#### _**The Deep End***_

#### _**The Deep End***_

_Tine tried to point at the table with one hand. “Now that I’ve learned to swim, sir, so to speak, I’d like to dive into the deep end. #### _**A Step Too Far***_

#### _**From Here To There***_

#### _**Seal the Deal***_
