"Hello, miss. We've got a delivery for a Lea—"

"Yes, yes, that's me." Lea brushed a strand of brown hair out of her face, signed for the delivery, and watched as two men heaved a large wooden crate into her living room.

"There you go. Have a good evening, miss." With that, she was alone again.

"What would Em's cousin want to send me?" she wondered aloud. In the end, her curiosity got the better of her. Lea removed the straps, levered open the lid and couldn't believe her eyes.
