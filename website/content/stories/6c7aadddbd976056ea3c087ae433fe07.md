Pushing open the heavy door, Christina stepped into the cool air of the small chapel, her footsteps echoing off the walls. Only a few candles burned, but their light filled the chamber with a warm glow.

Upon returning to the room, Christina took out her small book and began reading up on the cult. "The color red is considered holy…shackles and thorny roses are revered symbols…oh, ‘candles and hot wax are often used for ritualized punishment’, spicy!" she muttered, skimming through large sections before finally arriving at a chapter on arcane symbols.

"Ah, here it is," she exclaimed, slowly pacing around the room with her finger running over the words. "The cult of 'the Mistress' uses arcane circles to magically enforce contracts, typically centered around one person sexually submitting to another. The runes etched into the circle outline the boundaries and parameters of the contract, tailored to each agreement."

As Christina flipped through the various examples of arcane circles, a sense of deja vu washed over her. However, she couldn't quite pinpoint the source of the feeling until she turned to the next chapter and read on.

"The Mistress is also known as 'the Mother of the succubi.' She rescues lost souls with untapped potential from death's grasp, transforming those who accept her terms into succubi, half-demonic favored servants who revel in pleasure and overindulgence."

In that moment, everything clicked for Christina. The memories of her own contract came flooding back, overwhelming her with their intensity.

### Chapter 10: The deal

As memories flooded back to her, Christina found herself awash with vivid recollections. She remembered floating in the void and hearing a seductive voice in the darkness. "Consider this: you are dead, and what have you achieved in life? A mundane boring desk job and a lonely home? I can offer you something much better. Would you like me to send you back? If you ask me, I will do so. However, there is another option," the voice said.

With a flourish, the voice summoned a full-length mirror before Christina, revealing the stunningly naked form of herself as a succubus gazing back at her. "Become one of my daughters, spread desire and excess in my name, and I will ensure you remain eternally beautiful," the voice promised.

However, Christina wasn't ready to commit. Despite the cloud of desire that shrouded her mind, she was determined to find a catch in the offer made by the demon, asking questions and trying to stall the conversation. The voice seemed to sense her reluctance and presented another proposition.

"How about I show you what your potential new existence could be like? You can see for yourself and, if you're ready, you may submit to me or reject my proposal. If you choose to reject it, I will return you to your old life, unscathed and secure, with no strings attached."

Christina suddenly remembered the stunningly gorgeous succubus within the mirror. The succubus teasingly played with Christina's mirror image, while presenting the enticing proposal. The beauty was undoubtedly the most gorgeous creature Christina had ever laid eyes on, with luxurious long red hair and emerald green eyes.

### Chapter 11: Acceptance

"Laressa!" Christina exclaimed her lover's name, bringing her thoughts back to the present. She felt her legs growing weak as the realization hit her - her lover was none other than 'the Mistress,' the literal goddess of pleasure. "Laressa is the Mistress," she mumbled, articulating what her mind was gradually coming to accept.

"I have willingly submitted to her!" Christina then realized, as she pieced together the puzzle of her time in Edoria. "Before we had sex she asked me if i would like to let go and accept my new life and I said yes!"

Filled with a sudden sense of urgency, Christina rushed out of the temple and hurried through the bustling streets of the city. She skillfully maneuvered through the crowds, until she finally arrived at her lover's house.

However, instead of the lavish mansion Christina remembered, she found an old, run-down ruin. Christina slowly walked up to the building, gazing at it with disbelief. She vividly remembered it being a luxurious and well-maintained place, but now it appeared as though it had been abandoned and left to decay for years.

With the door collapsed, Christina had to climb through a window. Surveying the surroundings, she discovered the familiar rooms and even some of the furniture, left behind and forgotten for decades. After a brief tour, she returned to the sitting room, where she found a broken cup under a bit of rubble. She wondered if it was the one she had used during her conversation with Laressa.

Holding the broken piece of ceramic, she reminisced about her conversation with Laressa. "How about you, dear? Besides everything being confusing, do you really miss your old life?" she had asked, and as she slowly turned the old cup in her hand, Christina shook her head.

Despite the trick Laressa had played on her, Christina had to admit that the night with her had been the best she had ever experienced. If more like that lay ahead, she found herself not missing her old life. Discarding the broken cup, she walked up to the closest window and climbed out of the ruin.

"Maybe I should return to that tavern I found on my first night," she mused, dusting off her clothes. "Perhaps I can join Kael and his group. I think I would like to embark on some adventures with them," she added, stepping back onto the street with a smile on her face.
