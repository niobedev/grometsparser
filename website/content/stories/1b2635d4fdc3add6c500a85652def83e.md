As I looked around myself I couldn't help but think that this was a
strange place for me to be.  Here I am a scrawny 20 year old male
and I had just broken into a small bridal store. I had heard that they
kept fairly large amounts of cash here overnight. I was searching around
the cash register when I heard a noise behind me. Before I could react
I felt hands grab and hold me while a cloth was held over my nose and mouth.
There must have been something on the cloth because I soon passed out.

I don't know how long I was out, but when I came to I was in a very
unusual situation.  I was in a open area of the store with a large
curtain covering the wall in front of me. I was unable to move anything
but my head, and upon examining myself I found out why. I was laced so
tightly in a white satin corset that I could hardly breath. There were
white stockings attached to the garters of the corset, and I had on white
lace panties and bra complete with fairly large falsies. On my feet were
white high heels. Now for the reason I couldn't move. There appeared to
be a large heavily weighted stand with a steel rod going up behind my back.
I believe the corset was lace to the rod. My ankles were firmly strapped
to two rods coming off the base holding my legs about a foot apart and
about six inches in front of the base. My shoulders were taped to another
rod that ran behind my shoulders and down the back side of both arms all
the way to my hands.

My arms were taped to the rods at various spots completely immobilizing
them and holding them in a relaxed straight down position and then bringing
my hands almost together in front of me at my waist. Long white gloves
covered my hands and the rods all the way up to my elbows. I might also
add that I was gagged with a pair of panties (they were certainly not clean
judging by the taste they left in my mouth) and my mouth was taped shut.
I heard a noise and jerked my head back only to hit my head on the rod
that ran up behind my back all the way to my head. The noise turned out
to be my captors. They totally ignored my muffled pleas as one grabbed
my head and held it up against the rod while the other took a role of white
medical tape and began wrapping it around my head and the rod. In a very
short time my head was securely fastened to the rod. They then began tightly
wrapping up my neck and head. Totally mummifying them except for two tiny
holes for my nose and two tiny holes for my eyes.

They held up a mirror for me to see my self.  My body looked very
female with the bra, panties, corset, stockings, heels, and gloves, but
my head looked like a human head totally devoid of all features. If I really
squinted I could see the eye and nose holes, but they had wrapped my head
so tight that the tape looked a seamless mask covering my entire head and
neck. My captors disappeared for a couple of minutes only to reappear with
a very beautiful long sleeved high collared wedding gown that they held
up in front of me. They then lifted it over my head and brought it down
and proceeded to put it on me. They had a little problem getting my arms
into the sleeves, but eventually worked it up my arms and on to my shoulders.
Then after a few adjustments I could feel them pulling up the zipper from
the waist all the way up to the top of very high collar of the dress, and
thus sealing me into it.

The last thing they did was to put a veil on my head and tape a bouquet
of flowers in my hands. After a few adjustments to the veil and dress I
heard one of my captors say he looks perfect. The other one then said yes
but we sure cut it close on the time. None of this made any sense to me.
Why did they do this and what was the time limit they were working under,
but it all became very clear what my purpose was as they pulled back the
curtain in front of me. Daylight streamed in from the show room window
as I looked out over the busy street in front of me. The one lady said
to the other as they walked away "To bad we can't afford real mannequins."
