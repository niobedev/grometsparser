"I suppose not. You've probably never gotten involved with anyone, haven't you? What's that old saying? It is better to have loved and lost, then to have never loved at all? Hah. Whoever wrote that obviously didn't know what real loneliness is. The need to have someone to touch, hold, hug, and take care of is too strong to resist." My former employer looked at me. "I'm sorry Zan, but I just need somebody to take care of."

With a tug, all the chains were finished and in place. "I'll take good care of you." She said as she rummaged through a box. "But because of what I've done to you, I'm afraid that this situation may be permanent." Standing up, I saw that she was holding a leather hood. "I may never be able to release you."

She walked over to me, hood in hand. "I'm sorry Zan. I hope one day you can forgive me."

Without waiting any longer, she put the hood over my head. It was a very tight hood and it seemed to clutch at my head. There were no eyeholes, and there was no opening for the mouth. I was blind.

I felt my captor buckling the hood down, tightening the straps that ensured it would remain plastered to my head. When it was done, I felt something tight go around my neck and locked in place. It was a metal collar, and it firmly clasped my throat and the hood as well.

I was now in complete darkness. Blind, helpless, and unable to speak. Bound in latex and chains, at the mercy of my captor. With that, my imprisonment began…

Who was the mysterious lady in black?

Who was she indeed? I have had much time to ponder, and to think, and I think I know the answer to that question. The mysterious lady in black was, in a way, what we both wanted.

Mrs. Paz saw the mysterious lady in black as the being she could take care of, to insure that she wouldn't be lonely.

I saw her as the thing I wanted to become. I saw her form as what my heart wanted in life. To be submissive, to make someone happy.

But in a way, I am now the mysterious lady in black. How long have I been imprisoned in this suit, bound and restrained in a fashion that few can even imagine? I do not know. With my eyesight gone, I have no reference for day or night. It is always darkness in here, in this suit. Time has ceased to have any meaning for me.

I am kept chained in the spare bedroom in basement. The chain is just long enough for Mrs. Paz to move me around if she wants, but most of the time I lie on my bed. She spends most of her time with me, reading novels to me, changing my diet (she has hooked up some IV's into my arm), cleaning out the waste products box, or simply cuddling me. She constantly apologizes for what she's doing, that she has to keep me like this, most likely for the rest of my life.

Too bad I can't tell her that I'm having a blast.

I have had much time to think, to ponder, and to philosophize. Perhaps this new life isn't so bad after all.I have someone who takes care of me, who hugs and cuddles me, and who constantly works to make sure I'm never bored. What more could someone ask for?

Buried deep within this suit (from which I may never leave), I am sealed off from the world, yet I am in a new one all to my own. As time goes on, I find myself wanting to be put deeper and deeper into more restraints, more latex, until I am no longer a human being but a living latex suit.

Will my dream come true? Perhaps someday. Until then, I will simply have to bide my time and enjoy myself as the mysterious lady in black.
