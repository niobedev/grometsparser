“In the dollhouse…” Trisha said grimly, helping Chloe to her feet, whose legs were shaking. "I accidentally touched the door and was pulled inside. 

I just hope my sister finds us soon…“

”What if she doesn't?“

Trisha glanced briefly at Chloe and then turned away.

”From experience, I can say that at least we won't starve to death… but that's not necessarily good news…“ she added muttering and crossing her arms, ”at least that sadistic bitch is gone…"

 

“I'm back,” Kim called through the house and closed the door behind her. Carelessly, she threw her bag and jacket into a corner and jumped onto the sofa. She was about to turn on the TV when the magic crystal in her chest grew warm.

“Mhm?” Kim murmured and looked back at the living room door.

 Through the crystal, she could sense that someone in the house was playing with magic, and if it wasn't Celine or Rilliana, then who was it? She put down the remote control and followed the crystal's command up the stairs and finally to the attic. In front of her stood a dollhouse on the floor, which was clearly enchanted with the magic of her dark mistress.

“Mmm,” Kim said again, sitting down in front of it and running a finger over it. She could feel the presence of her sister and Trisha inside. They were prisoners of the dollhouse until they were released.

“Or until someone decides to have some fun with them,” Kim muttered, letting the magic of her crystal flow into her hand to activate the dollhouse. A broad grin spread across Kim's face and she licked her lips.
