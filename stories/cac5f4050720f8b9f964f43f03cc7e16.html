Bu-Shi-Doll stood above her apprentice for enough time for the girl to register the full horror of her mistake then dropped her wakizashi into the mud beside the kneeling girl.

‘Finish him.’

‘Hai.’ Katana Girl picked up the weapon and turned towards the dying man.

‘I punish you later.’ Bu-Shi-Doll turned away as Katana girl plunged the dagger into the man’s heart.
