"There you go. Have a good evening, miss." With that, she was alone again.

"What would Em's cousin want to send me?" she wondered aloud. In the end, her curiosity got the better of her. Lea removed the straps, levered open the lid and couldn't believe her eyes.

Inside the crate, facing each other, lay two women. Their bodies were pressed together and had been secured to its padded walls with thick leather straps, stifling any attempts at movement. The women were dressed in form-fitting black latex catsuits that immediately gave away that there were no further garments underneath. Their legs were folded back into a kneeling position and their upper bodies had been forced into heavy rubber straitjackets. Their faces were completely covered by masks, but Lea immediately recognized the long blonde hair protruding from one of them.

The two latex figures did not react to Lea's presence nor anything else for that matter, except to a soft buzzing sound emanating from between their legs, which elicited muffled moans from time to time.

Minutes passed while Lea stood there, dumbfounded, taking in the sight, until, at last, she burst out laughing.

“Seriously Em?!”
