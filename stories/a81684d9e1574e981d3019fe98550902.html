Following Lucy’s instructions, Barry then moved various items into the pool house to make it as unwelcoming as possible.

When they returned to the house Lucy was pleased with the mess the two girls had made.

Lucy called Lynn to her, Amy followed like a pet puppy, “Well done you two,“ she congratulated them, “now listen,“ she continued, “I will be leaving early in the morning and I’ve arranged it so John will be sleeping in the pool house. I’ve had you make this mess because I don’t want him sitting around all day tomorrow. So it’ll be up to both of you to keep him busy. Oh, and Lynn, I bought you a gift,” she gave Lynn a box two inches wide and two feet long. Lynn opened it and removed an ebony riding crop. She slipped the leather loop over her wrist and flicked the crop from side to side. A wide smile split her face.

“As I said, I want John busy. You two are to have as much fun as you can, make this weekend the most miserable he’s ever had,” Lucy instructed them. She took Lynn’s face between her two hands, looked into her eyes and told her “I bought you that crop for a reason, so use it. I want to see some nice welts on John’s body when I return. Now, he should be home soon, so take your new little pet,” she reached over and stroked Amy’s cheek and ran her thumb across her lips and into her mouth, Amy automatically sucked on the invading digit “and have a good night,” she told them.

* * *

By the time I had dropped Stephen and his clients off, and then managed to summon a taxi I had been out of the house almost one and a half hours. When I eventually arrived home the party was over. Lucy and Barry were the only people left in the house. Lucy relaxed on the sofa sipping wine, Barry was slouched in an armchair, he looked the worst for wear.

“Hello darling, you made it home safely then?” Lucy greeted me cheerfully “I’m afraid Barry has had a bit of a heavy evening. So I told him he could stay over tonight, and with the spare room still being full of stuff I thought it would be better if he slept in your room. You don’t mind do you? no of course you don’t, you’re such a dear. I thought you could sleep in the pool house. Its a nice night so you shouldn’t be cold”.

I was taken aback, this had come completely out of the blue. I tried to process it.

“But ..emm… why don’t I sleep on the sofa?” I asked, not understanding why I should give up my bed, but unable to put my thoughts into words.

“Oh no, just look at the mess,” Lucy said as if it were a ridiculous suggestion, “besides I have to leave early tomorrow and I wouldn’t want to disturb you. Now, I’ve poured you a scotch and I’m tired, so if you would give Barry a hand up the stairs we can all get some sleep,” she said and stood to show there would be no more discussion.

Reluctantly, I helped Barry to my room, and wishing my wife a good night I made my way to the pool house. I was stunned. I didn’t really know what had just happened. I had already been relegated to the spare bedroom, now suddenly I was no longer sleeping in my own house, while the bastard who had taken my job now slept in my bed. It never even occurred to me to suggest sleeping with Lucy. I merely did as she told me. Something that I had been doing more and more lately.

The pool house was a mess The bed was covered with junk. I wracked my brain trying to think how it got into such a state. looking back at the house I could see that all the lights were off, so Lucy was probably in bed. I knew I had better not disturb her, so with a sigh, I started to clear the bed. I had even forgotten to drink my whisky.
