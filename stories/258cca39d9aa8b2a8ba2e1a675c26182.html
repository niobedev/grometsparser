And because I know you would have fought tooth and nail against it, I asked Greed for help while you were asleep," Summer explained as she pressed Ava's arms behind her back and stuffed them into the sack. She sealed it with a zipper, forcing Ava's elbows to touch. Summer additionally secured the arm binders to her shoulders and put a wide belt around Ava's waist, constricting it like a corset.

“I think we'll skip the leg restraints, right?” Summer asked, but opened the zipper to expose Ava's legs. Immediately, a foot flew out, but Summer caught it effortlessly.

“Just kidding, of course,” Summer grinned wickedly, “Greed? How could you forget her shoes?”

Ava immediately grew several inches as Greed wiggled down her legs and slipped on ballet boots. She swayed dangerously until he helped her keep her balance. However, Summer wanted to make sure that Ava couldn't kick her again and fastened leather cuffs around her ankles and above her knees.

Finally, she tied a leash through Ava's collar and patted her head.

“You can let her go, Greed,” Summer said, and Ava flinched as she finally regained control of her body. However, the leather restraints meant she had no chance of taking revenge on Summer, so she had no choice but to stumble after the pink hair.

 

While Summer walked through the convention with wide eyes, examining the exhibits or the people testing them, Ava only allowed herself furtive glances.

“AVA! Look, look! You can get tied up here!” Summer called out, pulling her friend across the aisle to a large area where a woman in a spandex catsuit was trying to free herself from complicated rope restraints. While Summer cheered her on, Ava just peered at the stage. She would never admit it, but it looked quite interesting to see the woman writhing and trying to reach the knots with her fingers.

“Do you think I should try it?” Summer asked, glancing over her shoulder at Ava. She looked at Summer, then at the woman on the floor, then back at Summer. Ava nodded cautiously and bit her lip as Summer volunteered to be tied up next. What Ava hadn't considered was that she would now be pulled onto the stage, and she blushed when all eyes turned to her. Fortunately, Summer passed the leash on to a staff member at the booth and Ava was able to sit down on a stool in the shade. Meanwhile, Summer was tied up with ropes, to the cheers of the audience.

“I thought your friend wanted to sign you up for tying, but I see you're already taken care of,” the woman laughed, and Ava nodded shyly.

"Do you need something to drink? I could get you something," the woman offered, and Ava nodded again. She knew Greed would soon be hungry too, and didn't want him to get any silly ideas. The woman got up from her stool and went to a small tent to fetch a bottle of water. Meanwhile, Ava watched the rope master turn Summer into a helpless bundle and grinned behind her gag.

Her friend finally seemed to be back to her old self. Cheerful, full of life, and above all, full of nonsense.

“There you are! We've been looking everywhere for you!” said a woman, and Ava turned her head. Standing in front of her were three people who looked rushed, and the woman who had just spoken reached for Ava's leash.

Completely confused, they dragged Ava away from the stool for the first few steps until she realized what they were doing. She braced her legs against the ground and pulled back.

“What's going on, Carmen? You signed a contract!”

CARMEN?! It flashed through Ava's mind, and now she realized that these people had the wrong woman. Ava shook her head and braced herself against the leash. At the same time, she yelped into the gag, but the woman in particular didn't seem to care and just kept pulling. Ava sensed that Greed wanted to help her, but she growled into the gag to stop him. She didn't want to cause a mass panic by having a piece of clothing suddenly take on a life of its own.

“Honestly, Carmen! You don't have to be in character the whole time! We paid for a little acting, but this is really going too far,” said the woman, pulling a little harder on the leash so that Ava stumbled forward. A pair of strong hands grabbed her and carried her up a flight of stairs. Another push and Ava stumbled through a red curtain.

“And now, ladies and gentlemen! A big round of applause for Carmeeeeen Caramell!” Thunderous applause rang around Ava's ears, and a moment later she was blinded by spotlights. She immediately froze. In front of her were thousands of people. They all stared up at her expectantly, seeming to undress Ava with their eyes. The only thing Ava could do was stare back helplessly as her head grew redder and her mouth suddenly became so dry that it felt like she had eaten sand. The audience saw her as nothing more than a piece of meat, and that's how she felt. Humiliated and defenseless.

“Come on, Carmen! You can do it, escape from your bonds!” said the commentator, trying to help her somehow when he noticed that Ava wasn't moving. Ava looked over at him for help and shrugged her shoulders.

“Looks like Carmen's having trouble! Can we cheer her on?”

Immediately, the hall cheered Carmen's name, and Ava just wanted to sink into the ground, feeling Greed laughing up his sleeve.
