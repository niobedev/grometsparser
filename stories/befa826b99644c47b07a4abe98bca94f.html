Dan pulled it back out of her reach. “I’ll just keep it at my place. You won’t be needing anything in there before Tuesday. He looked her up and down. “And remember, no clothing till tomorrow when you go to work. That means not so much as a bath towel.”

Alice scowled. “Can I at least get under the covers in my bed?” she asked sarcastically.

“What time do you usually go to bed?”

Alice’s eyes widened. He was seriously telling her what time she could get into bed. “Uh, ten o’clock usually. I wasn’t serious, you know?”

Dan took her chin between his finger and thumb. He leaned in close. Inches away from her face he whispered, “It’s all serious now, Princess.” If she was going to give a response he stifled it with his lips on hers.
